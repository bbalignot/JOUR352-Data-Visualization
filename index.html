Complaints filed with Navient: where are the victims commonly found?

By Benicio Balignot


For years, the Navient Corporation, one of the nation's largest student loan services, has been embroiled in controversy and legal trouble stemming from an ever-growing amount of lawsuits.

In light of this, most of the complaints were found to have been filed as (from most common to least common) issues with:

  • Student loans
  • Credit reporting, credit repair services, and/or other personal consumer reports
  • Debt collection
USA Map
Infogram

From the data above, it's evident that most of the complaints with Navient regarding student loans come from high-population density states. This may be because these states have universities and colleges that attract students from across the country, meaning that there are more students that have to take out student loans to pay for out-of-state tuition. This may also be due to the correlation between urban areas and how much more of the population there seeks a higher education, as opposed to more rural regions.
